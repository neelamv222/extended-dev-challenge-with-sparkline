Understanding and Assumptions:

Assignments consist of two parts:

Part 1: A Sorted table:

According to my understanding in Part 1 which is "Sorted Table" we have to show currency pairs in a table with a single row for each unique currency pair and include columns for the following

  1. Name
  2. the current best bid price
  3. current best ask price
  4. the amount that the best bid last changed
  5. the amount the best price last changed

So I did the following in part 1:

  • Firstly, remove the duplicate currency pair and show only the one with highest "best bid last changed" value.
  • After this, sort above result set again based on the "best bid last changed" in descending order
Part 2: Sparkline:

In the second part, we have to draw a graph (sparkline) in the last column for each row. So, my graph (sparkline) will get display on the screen after 30 secs. And thereafter, the graph will keep on updating over the most recent data that comes from the server. The sparkline is calculated as the midprice over the 30 seconds. The midprice is calculated by adding the bestBid and bestAsk fields together and dividing by 2 as mentioned in the assignment.
